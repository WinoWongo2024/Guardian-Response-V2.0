Latest Service Updates

Current Disruptions

Ongoing track renewal works are causing delays across multiple lines. We’re working through the night to restore full service as quickly as possible.

  • Bakerloo line between Paddington and Queen’s Park: Replacement buses every 12–15 minutes until 23:30.
  • Piccadilly line lift outage at Covent Garden: Step-free assistance available on request.
  • Jubilee line signal upgrades: Minor delays expected; engineering teams on site 24/7.

Passenger Convenience

To keep you moving smoothly, we’re offering:

  • Free bus and tram transfers on impacted sections — tap your Oyster or contactless card as usual.
  • Enhanced station staff presence for wayfinding and step-free guidance.
  • Real-time updates via the TfL Go app, station displays, and social channels.
  • 24/7 journey planning support: call 0343 222 1234 or visit any station help desk.
